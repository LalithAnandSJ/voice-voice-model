Edu-Vox

Your Voice-Powered Educational Assistant

Click the microphone to start...

How to Use:

  1. Select a learning mode from the dropdown
  2. Click the microphone button and speak clearly
  3. Wait for Edu-Vox to process and respond
  4. Press Space to toggle the microphone

Edu-Vox runs locally with TinyLLaMA (1.1B) for privacy and offline use.
